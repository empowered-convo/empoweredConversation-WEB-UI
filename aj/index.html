How does Empowered Conversation work?
When a survivor is ready to disclose about their assault,
they go onto our website and input the cell phone number of the person they will disclose to.

Why is our approach different?

For the survivor

Our tool takes the burden off of the survivor for having to educate their loved ones about assault during disclosure because we provide that training ahead of time.

It allows the survivor to feel more empowered in the conversation, knowing their loved one already has an understanding of assault and best practices for how to support them.

For the family member/friend

Our tool allows time for the family member/friend to integrate the information and process their own emotions such as their own shock, pain and denial, the pain and grief of the survivor.

When they respond to the survivor they are better able to empower and support in a sensitive and empathetic way.
